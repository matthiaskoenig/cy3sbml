Short message    :	Invalid value for the 'units' attribute of a three-dimensional compartment
Full message     :	The value of the 'units' attribute on a <compartment> having 'spatialDimensions' of '3' is restricted. 
Reference: L2V1 Section 4.5.4
 The value of the 'units' attribute on a <compartment> having 'spatialDimensions' of '3' must be either 'volume', 'litre', or the identifier of a <unitDefinition> based on either 'litre', 'metre' (with 'exponent' equal to '3').
