Short message    :	Invalid value for the 'units' attribute of a one-dimensional compartment
Full message     :	The value of the 'units' attribute on a <compartment> having 'spatialDimensions' of '1' is restricted. 
 The value of the 'units' attribute on a <compartment> having 'spatialDimensions' of '1' must be either 'length' or 'metre', or the identifier of a <unitDefinition> based on either 'metre' (with 'exponent' equal to '1'). The <compartment> with id 'c' does not comply.
