Short message    :	Invalid value for the 'units' attribute of a two-dimensional compartment
Full message     :	The value of the 'units' attribute on a <compartment> having 'spatialDimensions' of '2' is restricted.
Reference: L2V1 Section 4.5.4
 The value of the 'units' attribute on a <compartment> having 'spatialDimensions' of '2' must be either 'area' or the identifier of a <unitDefinition> based on 'metre' (with 'exponent' equal to '2'). The <compartment> with id 'c' does not comply.
