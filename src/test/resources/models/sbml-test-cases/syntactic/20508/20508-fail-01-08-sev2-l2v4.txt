Short message    :	Invalid value for the 'units' attribute of a two-dimensional compartment
Full message     :	The value of the 'units' attribute on a <compartment> having 'spatialDimensions' of '2' is restricted.
Reference: L2V4 Section 4.7.5
 The value of the 'units' attribute on a <compartment> having 'spatialDimensions' of '2' must be either 'area', 'dimensionless', or the identifier of a <unitDefinition> based on either 'metre' (with 'exponent' equal to '2') or 'dimensionless'. The <compartment> with id 'c' does not comply.
