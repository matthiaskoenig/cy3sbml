Full message     :	Redefinitions of the built-in unit 'substance' are restricted.
Reference: L2V1 Section 4.4.3
 Redefinitions of the built-in unit 'substance' must be based on the units 'mole' or 'item'. More formally, a <unitDefinition> for 'substance' must simplify to a single <unit> whose 'kind' attribute has a value of 'mole' or 'item', and whose 'exponent' attribute has a value of '1'.
