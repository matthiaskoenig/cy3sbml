Short message    :	Cannot have a reaction with neither reactants nor products
Full message     :	A <reaction> definition must contain at least one <speciesReference>, either in its <listOfReactants> or its <listOfProducts>. A reaction without any reactant or product species is not permitted, regardless of whether the reaction has any modifier species.
